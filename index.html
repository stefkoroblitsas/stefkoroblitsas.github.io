Rock Climbing

Sidebar

The map is built upon the Leaflet.js library, a leading open-source JavaScript library for mobile-friendly interactive maps. Its simplicity, performance, and usability make it a go-to solution for developers. The base maps utilized in this project are sourced from OpenStreetMap and World Imagery, providing a detailed and contrasting view of the terrains.

A variety of plugins enhance the map's functionality and user experience:

  • Leaflet Search Plugin: Enables users to easily search and zoom into specific points of interest on the map, making navigation intuitive and efficient.
  • Leaflet Geoman Plugin: Provides drawing and editing functionalities, empowering users to interact with the map in a meaningful way.
  • Leaflet Sidebar Plugin: Facilitates a clean and collapsible sidebar, housing extra information and settings without cluttering the map view.
  • Chroma.js: Aids in generating a dynamic color scheme for different categories of points, enhancing the visual appeal and interpretability of the map.
  • GeoJSON: The geographical data representing the climbing routes and movie locations is structured in GeoJSON format, a type of open standard format designed for representing simple geographical features, along with their non-spatial attributes.
